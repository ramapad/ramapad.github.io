About Me

I am broadly interested in measuring networks and am actively working on measuring Internet addressing, topology, and connectivity.
Before joining CAIDA in 2019, I completed my Ph.D. in Computer Science from the University of Maryland, where I worked with Dr. Neil Spring and Dr. Dave Levin. My dissertation focused upon measuring residential Internet connectivity using active probes.

At CAIDA, I am continuing to develop methodologies to detect Internet connectivity outages as part of the IODA project with Dr. Alberto Dainotti.
